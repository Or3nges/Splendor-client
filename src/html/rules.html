Rules

Contents:

40x tokens

7x Emerald tokens

7x Diamond tokens

7x Onyx tokens

7x Ruby tokens

7x Sapphire tokens

5x Gold tokens

Contents:

90x development cards

40x level 1 cards

30x level 2 cards

20x level 3 cards

10x noble cards

Development cards:

Development cards are divided into 3 levels.

Level 1 cards will not give any prestige points, but will be cheaper to purchase.

Level 2 cards will give between 1 or 3 prestige points and will cost more than level 1 cards.

Level 3 cards will give between 3 and 5 and will cost the most tokens.

Upon purchasing a card, you will get an additional discount according to the color of the card for any future purchases.

Noble cards:

Noble cards will give you 3 prestige points if you meet the requirements.

Each noble card has a different requirement.

If you meet the requirement to take 2 nobles cards. You will have to pick one.

Game setup:

4 random cards per level will be revealed alongside 5 noble cards if the game has 4 players.

4 noble cards will be revealed if the game has 3 players.

3 noble cards will be revealed if the game has 2 players.

Gameplay:

Players take turns to perform one of the following actions:

  • Take 3 different tokens
  • Take 2 tokens of the same type
  • Reserve a card and take 1 gold token
  • Buy a card with the collected tokens

Win condition:

When a player reaches 15 prestige points, the game ends and the player with the most prestige points wins.
